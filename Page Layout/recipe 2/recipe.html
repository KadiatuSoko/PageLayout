Cassava Leaf

Cassava leaf soup also known as saka saka or pondu is a simple, yet tasty and substantial soup that is widely consumed in many parts of Central Africa especially in countries like Sierra Leone, and Liberia

Ingredients:

You will not regret this recipe

  • 2 pounds of Cassava Leafs
  • 2 pound of beef smoke
  • 3 table sppons of groundnut
  • Maggie powder, pepper, onions and salt
  • Palm oil


Directions:

  1. In a large saucepan, season meat with salt, Maggi and onions and boil until tender, depending on the choice of meat. You should have at least 1-2 cups of stock. Remove the meat and beef stock, reserve
  2. Heat oil in a large sauce pan over medium heat , add onions until fragrant about 1-2 minutes. Add beef and or / smoked meat
  3. Stir in peanut butter or groundnut paste, then add stock about a cup cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Throw in the drained cassava leaves and continue cooking for about 25 more minutes
  5. Serve warm over rice

This is my favorite recipe because it taste good, it's healthy, and it's one of our favorite dish in Sierra Leonne.
